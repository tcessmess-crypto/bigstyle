Our Product Range

Welcome to a collection where fit is never an afterthought. We specialise in providing apparel specifically engineered for bigger and taller men, eliminating the frustration of clothes that are too short, tight, or ill-proportioned. Our entire range—from essential basics to sharp formal wear—is crafted with extra length, broader proportions, and proper tailoring, ensuring exceptional comfort and a confident, perfect fit every time.

Shirts & Tops

From crisp business shirts that maintain their tuck to casual polos and ultra-comfortable t-shirts, our shirts are engineered with extra length and broader proportions. This ensures a comfortable fit across the chest and torso without being too short, providing the perfect fit for bigger and taller men.

Pants & Jeans

Our comprehensive pants collection includes sophisticated dress trousers, versatile casual chinos, durable denim jeans, and practical cargo pants. Every pair is available in extended sizes and crafted with proper rise and inseam measurements, eliminating the discomfort of a restricted crotch or high-water cuffs.

Outerwear

Stay warm and stylish in our collection of jackets, coats, hoodies, and blazers. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to fit comfortably across the shoulders and chest while providing the crucial body and sleeve length you need, ensuring a great silhouette that doesn't feel tight or look small.

Suits & Formal Wear

Look sharp and feel confident in our tailored suits and formal wear collection. Perfect for business meetings, weddings, and special occasions, our expert tailoring and quality fabrics ensure a truly polished appearance and a fit that flatters your frame perfectly.

Footwear & Accessories

Complete your outfit effortlessly with our selection of shoes in extended sizes that don't compromise on style or comfort. Our accessories—including belts, ties, and socks—are sized appropriately to match your proportions.

Custom Fitting Service

Not sure about your size? Visit our Brisbane CBD store for a professional fitting consultation. Our experienced staff will help you find the perfect size and can arrange alterations if needed to ensure your clothes fit perfectly.
